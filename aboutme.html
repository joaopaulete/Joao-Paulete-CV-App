I’m a motivated and responsible person with an ambition and will power that made me change a good job in Lisbon and go to London looking for a better one, or leave the events area after 6 years and go looking for something better and challenging in web development.

I believe that these are some of the qualities that I consider essential to have in any worker of any company. I know that I am a person that brings that and much more to the table and what I lack in experience, I make up for in willingness to learn and evolve.

Working for 5 years at Lisbon Animated Film Festival, a festival with a small team and even smaller budget allowed me to enhance my capability of multi-tasking and to make things done with few resources, allowing me to be a more organised and efficient person. This experience made me do and learn things from other areas like editing video and image with software that I wasn’t acquainted and with this I understood that without sacrifice nothing could be accomplished.

I’m a driven person that goes after what I want, being able to work with and coordinate a small team of 3 or a big team of 20 employees or even a team of 50 festival volunteers. Working under pressure doesn’t scare me, “au contraire” I believe that brings out the best of me.
